Sum Even Or Odd

Write a program to check whether the sum of two no is even or odd .

Example 1:

Input:

65 23

Output: Even

Explanation: Sum of 65+23=88 => Even.

Example 2:

Input:

-133 72

Output: Odd

Explanation: Sum of -133+72=-61 => Odd.

Constraints:

2^-30 <= n <= 2^30
